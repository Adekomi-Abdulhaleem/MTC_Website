Our School

M uslim Teens College was established in January 1985. As a result of our rich curriculum and the excellent academic performance of our students over the years, our school has now become one of the most sought-after international schools in Alimosho and all over Nigeria.

At Muslim Teens College, we understand how boys and girls grow and learn.Therefore, we use a modern curriculum that makes learning easy for our students. Furthermore, all our teachers are certified, and our classes are equipped with state-of-art learning facilities.

Although we are one of the few Muslim schools in Nigeria that strictly run a Islamic curriculum, we are proudly Muslims. While we train our students to become global citizens, we celebrate the values and heritage of Nigeria.
